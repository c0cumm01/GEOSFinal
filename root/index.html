Introduction

What is the Biodiversity Crisis? The current Biodiversity crisis Earth is in includes rapid loss of species and the equally rapid destruction of Ecosystems. How did we get here? Our actions as humans lead to the Biodiversity crisis. The three main causes being overexploitation of natural resources, deforestation, and habitat destruction, and climate change.

Feed Back Loops

The Primary loop is the economy, and our day-to-day systems are built upon the human actions that cause the crisis. Following this pattern, our advancement is also the advancement of the Biodiversity Crisis.
